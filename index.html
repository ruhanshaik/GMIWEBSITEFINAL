Project timelines vary based on complexity. A standard website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex applications may take 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Absolutely! Startups and small businesses are our primary focus. We understand budget constraints and offer flexible solutions that scale with your growth.

We work with modern technologies including React, Node.js, Android, iOS, cloud platforms, and AI tools. We choose the best stack based on your specific project needs.

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance, security updates, performance monitoring, and feature additions to ensure your digital products stay current and secure.
