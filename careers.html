AI Intern

Stipend Based

As an AI Intern at GMI Tech, you will work alongside our engineering team to build and integrate AI/ML models into real-world products. You'll gain hands-on experience with LLMs, automation pipelines, and AI-powered features.

Responsibilities

  • Research and prototype AI/ML features for client products
  • Integrate LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) into web applications
  • Build and test automation workflows powered by AI
  • Collaborate with developers and designers on AI-first features
  • Document AI implementations and findings clearly

  • Interest in LLMs, NLP, and generative AI tools
  • Strong problem-solving mindset and eagerness to learn

Stipend Based
